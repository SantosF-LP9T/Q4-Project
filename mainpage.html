What's Next?

As an ICT student, I think that next year will be tougher than ever. ICT is one the subjects where it's really hard for me to understand but I will try my best anyways. Even through the hardships, ICT is the few subjects where I truly look to others for their help and advice, where we can collaborate and share ideas and thoughts. That is why I expect that next year, there will be many collaborations between classmates, guidance between friends, and learning among all students. Additionally, I look forward to how much more I can design my projects as ICT allows me to show my interests very well. To be honest, I am not sure what we will do next year but as long as I am having fun and finding enjoyment in it, I think all will be well.
